PRODUCT NAME:OLIGEN

The Orijen product line includes six dry dog foods,
all of them puptastic. We love that it contains herring
(a fatty marine fish naturally high in protein),
as well as omega-3 fatty acids and essential oils.
These are things needed by every dog to
sustain life. It’s got tons of great proteins
(including eggs, which are super easy to digest and have an
exceptionally high biological value)
and – a personal favorite – probiotics.
Just like they do for us, probiotics work
to enhance a dog’s digestive and
immune functions.

PRODUCT NAME:instinct

The Nature’s Variety Instinct product line includes five dry dog foods,
and while all of them are great (and five star rated),
we especially love this formula. Lots of good protein
(from concentrate) and a few interesting
and great ingredients (tapioca, canola oil, pumpkin seeds and alfalfa meal).
We also love the use of inulin, a starch-like
compound made up of repeating units of
carbohydrates and typically sourced from chicory root.
It’s both a natural source of soluble dietary
fiber and a prebiotic used to promote the growth of healthy
bacteria in a dog’s digestive tract.
